Born and brought up in Johannesburg, South Africa, Vinosh had a challenging and tedious schooling career. Toward the final years of his completion, Vinosh earned the title as "Mr. Bollywood" due to entering in the Mr. & Miss High School pageant which earned him the title "2nd runner" up 2 years in a row.




Shortly after High School, Vinosh took it further and joined a Modeling Academy. There he acquired the skill and confidence earning him the titles "Model of the Year" and "Face Of Espoir". Upon joining the agency, he also attended many auditions and appeared in a few castings with success. Along one unsuccessful audition, Vinosh developed an interest in The King Of Pop, Michael Jackson (MJ). In every effort to try and enter the audition again, it was too late. Nevertheless Vinosh then took the interest in MJ further. Learning MJ's moves to precision and performing them in front of friends and families at events and functions while gaining sheer confidence.

Vinosh was then approached by a friend who introduced him to the manager of a restaurant. He was offered a job as a waiter who slowly took an interest in Djing as the restaurant had its own equipment. Upon Djing and performing, this is where his popularity began to grow. Many other artists performed there. That was where Vinosh then met Suraj.


Vinosh was offered, and then featured in Suraj's show "Junoon...The Craze" a musical drama. Vinosh performed a role as a robber and did a dance item from the movie "36 China Town". A while after, Vinosh then purchased his own DJ equipment and moved on to doing private functions, thrilling his smaller audiences. Suraj then gave Vinosh the opportunity of doing a tribute to Michael Jackson in his show "Yaadein...Down Memory Lane" which took Vinosh to a higher level of exposure. Upon gaining this exposure, Vinosh then performed at many functions and tribute shows.

Vinosh also appeared in Tributes to MJ events hosted by clubs. He attended one of which was hosted by Black Orchid. Vinosh approached the club owner, "Mr. T", and had become one of the resident DJ's. Vinosh then continued to impress audiences in Johannesburg, Durban and Cape Town with tributes to Michael Jackson as well as DJ in various clubs.


Vinosh had teamed up with Suraj and Sitaare Dance Academy for numerous shows featuring Bollywood Stars. He enjoys learning new aspects of showbiz and has tried his hand at most parts already. A recent (2014) sucessful attempt at movie making earned him the titles actor, director, etc. in the short film "Let It All Out". As a DJ and music composer, Vinosh has featured mixes on Lotus FM's Drive Time "Phat Jam".
